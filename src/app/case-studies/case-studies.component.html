TrueChain used as the infrastructure for digital assets

TrueChain is not only building an infrastructure for dApps, but also building an infrastructure for global assets. Recently TrueChain incubated Pandaq, with 8 Decimal Capital. Through the asset tokenization platform, 8 homes on the beautiful Ko Samui Island were tokenized in April 2019. More assets are lined up to be tokenized on TrueChain.

First public chain with multiple coins and access to exchanges

TrueChain has reached coorperation with MakerDao stable coin and will issue TrueChain stablecoin Dai in the near future. TrueChain will work with the top stable coin projects like Pax, TrueUSD and many more to issue stable coins on TrueChain. The TrueChain version of each stablecoin will be traded on exchanges and OTC.
